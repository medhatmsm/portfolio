What is the structure of your dataset?¶

We can see that the report of each year contains more than 7 millions obervations with a 29 columns of variables describing a lot of details about flights, such as: flight's date, origin & destinantion, planned / actual time of departure / arrival, if any cancellation / delays are recorded along with the reason's of cancellation / delay and some other details.

What is/are the main feature(s) of interest in your dataset?¶

In this report, I'm interested in exploring the carrier delay which is by definition: "A delay due to circumstances within the airline's control (e.g. maintenance or crew problems, aircraft cleaning, baggage loading, fueling, etc.)".

What features in the dataset do you think will help support your investigation into your feature(s) of interest?¶

I expect that the CarrierDelay will be affected by few features like Year & Origin.

To start, we will combine the datasets of 2006,2007 & 2008 so we can check the changes over the years, and we will filter out the duplicates and irrelevant features to end up with a lighter dataset.
